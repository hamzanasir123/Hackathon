# Hardware Specifications

This appendix provides detailed specifications for the robotic platforms and hardware components referenced throughout the course.

## Humanoid Robot Platforms

### NAO Robot
- **Height**: 58 cm
- **Weight**: 5.2 kg
- **Degrees of Freedom**: 25
- **Processors**: Dual-core CPU, GPU
- **Sensors**: 2 cameras, 2 microphones, 9 tactile sensors, 20 pressure sensors
- **Connectivity**: WiFi, Ethernet, Bluetooth
- **Battery Life**: 90 minutes typical operation

### Pepper Robot
- **Height**: 120 cm
- **Weight**: 28 kg
- **Degrees of Freedom**: 20
- **Processors**: Intel Atom, NVIDIA GPU
- **Sensors**: 3D depth sensor, 2 HD cameras, 3 microphones, touch sensors
- **Connectivity**: WiFi, 4G (optional)
- **Battery Life**: 12 hours in idle, 2 hours active use

### Atlas Robot (Boston Dynamics)
- **Height**: 1.75 m (standing)
- **Weight**: 82 kg
- **Actuators**: 28 hydraulic and electric
- **Sensors**: Stereo cameras, LIDAR, IMU
- **Power**: 3.5 kW generator
- **Capabilities**: Bipedal locomotion, manipulation

## Simulation Platforms

### NVIDIA Isaac Sim
- **Minimum Requirements**:
  - GPU: NVIDIA GTX 1060 or equivalent
  - RAM: 16 GB
  - Storage: 20 GB free space
- **Recommended Requirements**:
  - GPU: NVIDIA RTX 3080 or equivalent
  - RAM: 32 GB
  - Storage: 50 GB SSD

### Gazebo Compatibility
- **Supported ROS Versions**: ROS 1 Melodic/Noetic, ROS 2 Foxy/Dashing/Humble
- **Physics Engines**: ODE, Bullet, Simbody, DART
- **Graphics**: OpenGL 3.3+ compatible GPU

## Development Hardware

### Recommended Development Setup
- **CPU**: Intel i7 or AMD Ryzen 7 (8+ cores)
- **RAM**: 32 GB minimum, 64 GB recommended
- **GPU**: NVIDIA RTX series for AI/ML tasks
- **Storage**: 1 TB SSD for optimal performance
- **Network**: Gigabit Ethernet, 802.11ac WiFi

## Sensor Specifications

### RGB-D Cameras
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (color), 640x480 (depth)
- **Frame Rate**: 30 FPS
- **Depth Range**: 0.3m to 10m
- **Accuracy**: ±3mm at 1m distance

### LIDAR Systems
- **Range**: 0.15m to 30m
- **Accuracy**: ±2cm
- **Angular Resolution**: 0.09° to 0.36°
- **Scan Rate**: 5-20 Hz

:::note
Specifications may vary by manufacturer and model. Always verify current specifications before purchasing hardware for your projects.
:::
