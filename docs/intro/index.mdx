# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive course on **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**. This course explores the fascinating intersection of digital intelligence and physical embodiment, providing you with the knowledge and skills needed to understand and develop intelligent robotic systems.

## Course Overview

This course is structured into 4 core modules that progressively build your understanding of humanoid robotics:

1. **Module 1**: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)
2. **Module 2**: The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

- Understand the fundamental principles of Physical AI
- Design and implement robotic control systems
- Create digital twins for robot simulation
- Develop AI algorithms for robotic perception and action
- Integrate vision, language, and action systems in humanoid robots

## Prerequisites

This course assumes familiarity with:

- Basic programming concepts
- Linear algebra and calculus fundamentals
- Introduction to robotics concepts (helpful but not required)
